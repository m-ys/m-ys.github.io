This is the final portfolio of M-Y S. for the course Art of the Web, taken in the Fall semester of 2023. All projects hyperlink to external webpages.

The galaxy tile used on this website and in project 1.3 was self-created in Adobe Photoshop, as were the animations for project 3.1. Other images, gifs, and videos were downloaded and modified from users on Pexels and Unsplash. Icons used in Projects 1.3 and 2.2 are from Icons8 and Linustock.


A big thank you to the instructor for their kindness and guidance this past semester!
